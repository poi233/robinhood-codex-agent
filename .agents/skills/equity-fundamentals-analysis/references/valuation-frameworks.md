# Valuation Frameworks

## Fit Valuation to the Business

Do not force one method onto every company.

## Common Lenses

- earnings multiple: mature businesses with reasonably clean earnings
- free cash flow yield or DCF: cash-generative businesses
- EV/EBITDA or EV/sales: capital structure or reinvestment can distort net income
- asset value or liquidation logic: balance-sheet-driven situations
- scenario range: volatile, cyclical, or uncertain businesses

## What to Avoid

- valuing capital-intensive low-quality businesses on peak earnings
- valuing unprofitable businesses as if growth alone proves eventual value
- ignoring dilution, debt, or financing needs

## Practical Rule

Choose the main method, then use one backup method as a reasonableness check.

## Valuation Output

Always state:

- why this method fits the business
- what assumptions matter most
- what would make the valuation wrong
