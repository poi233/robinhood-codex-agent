# Balance Sheet Risk

## Why It Matters

A company can look fine on the income statement and still be structurally fragile.

## Key Risk Areas

- net debt
- refinancing needs
- interest burden
- working-capital pressure
- customer advances that may reverse
- covenant sensitivity
- off-balance-sheet obligations where relevant

## Good Balance Sheet Traits

- manageable debt
- ample liquidity
- room to survive a downturn
- financing not required just to sustain the story

## Weak Balance Sheet Traits

- high leverage tied to optimistic assumptions
- short runway if growth slows
- dependence on capital markets access
- obligations that make equity claims secondary to survival

## Practical Rule

When downside is being considered, the balance sheet often matters more than the headline P/E.
