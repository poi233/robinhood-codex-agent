# Ticker Case Map

## Purpose

This file maps the core research universe to the most useful fundamental casebook lenses, so future updates land in the right place quickly.

## AAPL

Primary teaching uses:

- high-quality compounder
- capital allocation
- earnings quality as a positive control
- valuation discipline in a premium quality business

Use AAPL when the lesson is about:

- durable ecosystem economics
- strong cash conversion
- shareholder-friendly capital return
- the difference between a great business and a stretched entry

## AVGO

Primary teaching uses:

- cyclical-and-semi quality
- capital allocation through acquisition
- valuation trap after perfection pricing
- earnings strength versus market-expectation risk

Use AVGO when the lesson is about:

- exceptional economics inside a cyclical industry
- integration and leverage tradeoffs
- how strong numbers can still lead to a weak stock

## NVDA

Primary teaching uses:

- high-quality compounder in semis
- growth quality
- valuation sensitivity without low business quality
- balance-sheet strength in a capital-intensive ecosystem

Use NVDA when the lesson is about:

- real infrastructure demand
- platform economics
- premium expectations attached to elite execution

## TSLA

Primary teaching uses:

- valuation trap
- narrative versus economics
- margin fragility
- capital allocation judged against ambitious expectations

Use TSLA when the lesson is about:

- story strength outrunning current economics
- the gap between excitement and cash reality
- why high multiple names need stricter earnings-quality discipline
