# Edge Cases

## Case 1: Fast Growth, Mixed Quality

Observed setup:
- revenue grows quickly
- margins unstable
- cash conversion weak

Why it was tricky:
- narrative looked strong
- economics were not yet proven

Operational takeaway:
- growth without proof of quality deserves a lower confidence rating

## Case 2: Cheap Multiple, Hidden Fragility

Observed setup:
- stock appears optically cheap
- leverage and refinancing risk are underappreciated

Operational takeaway:
- low multiple can be a warning, not an opportunity
