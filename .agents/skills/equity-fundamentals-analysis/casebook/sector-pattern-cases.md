# Sector Pattern Cases

## Semiconductors

- is growth structural or cyclical?
- are margins driven by product edge or temporary shortage?

## Consumer

Questions:

- repeat behavior or fad demand?

## Industrials

Questions:
- cycle sensitivity?
- order quality?
- aftermarket resilience?

## Financial-Like Businesses

Questions:
- leverage?
- funding model?
- asset quality?
- spread durability?
