# Failure Cases

## Case 1: Story Stock With Weak Cash Reality

Observed setup:
- management sells a large market story
- reported growth is strong
- free cash flow and dilution tell a weaker story

Why it failed:
- investors mistook narrative scale for economic proof

Operational takeaway:
- separate addressable market excitement from shareholder economics

## Case 2: Acquisition-Driven EPS Mirage

Observed setup:
- EPS keeps growing
- acquisitions and adjusted metrics do most of the work

Why it failed:
- apparent quality was accounting and deal-driven, not organic

Operational takeaway:
- per-share value creation matters more than headline adjusted EPS
