# Standard Cases

## Case 1: High-Quality Compounder

Context:
- recurring demand
- stable margins
- strong cash conversion
- disciplined capital allocation

Correct interpretation:
- quality deserves attention before arguing over headline multiple

Operational takeaway:
- great businesses are often expensive for real reasons

## Case 2: Healthy Cyclical

Context:
- earnings move with cycle
- balance sheet remains manageable
- management behaves rationally through downturns

Correct interpretation:
- cyclicality does not equal low quality if resilience exists
