# Failure Cases

## Case 1: Chasing the Top of a Range

Observed setup:
- trader buys a strong bull close
- breakout is still inside a larger trading range

Why it failed:
- not enough room before resistance
- larger range logic dominated

Operational takeaway:
- buy pullbacks, not emotional closes, near range extremes

## Case 2: Holding Trend Logic After Overlap Returns

Observed setup:
- trade gets initial follow-through
- later bars overlap and alternate
- trader still targets measured move

Why it failed:
- regime already downgraded back to range

Operational takeaway:
- price action can revoke the original thesis
