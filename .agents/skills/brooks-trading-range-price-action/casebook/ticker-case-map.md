# Ticker Case Map

## SPY

Best used for:

- breakout pullbacks
- session regime shifts
- trend-to-range downgrade
- range-to-trend transitions in index context

## BTC-USD

Best used for:

- failed breakouts
- violent repair rallies
- trend exhaustion and continuation confusion

## AVGO

Best used for:

- failed breakout fades after earnings
- gap-driven regime change
- bounce quality after structural breaks

## NVDA

Best used for:

- leader pullbacks
- weak bounce versus true re-acceleration
- trend damage in high-quality growth names

## AAPL

Best used for:

- orderly breakout pullbacks
- repair rallies in quality mega-cap names

## TSLA

Best used for:

- failed breakouts in high-beta names
- range-to-trend transition failure
- emotional momentum traps
