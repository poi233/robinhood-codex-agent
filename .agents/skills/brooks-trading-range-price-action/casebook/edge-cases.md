# Edge Cases

## Case 1: Strong Bull Spike Into Range Ceiling

Why it was tricky:
- bars looked trend-like
- location still favored failure

Correct interpretation:
- treat as breakout test of the larger range, not automatic bull trend

## Case 2: Reversal Bar After Climactic Move

Why it was tricky:
- reversal shape looked obvious
- trend context still strong enough to punish early fading

Operational takeaway:
- strong countertrend ideas often need a second signal
