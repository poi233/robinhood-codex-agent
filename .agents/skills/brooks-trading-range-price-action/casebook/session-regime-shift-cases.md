# Session Regime Shift Cases

## Case 1: SPY 15m, 2026-06-12

Pattern tags:
- `session-regime-shift`
- `trend-to-range-downgrade`

Context:
- morning strength broke upward cleanly
- midday bars began to overlap after the push toward `744.44`

Correct interpretation:
- early session was trend-like
- later session reverted toward range logic

Operational takeaway:
- session regime can change before the day is done; the trader has to downgrade the thesis when overlap returns

## Case 2: SPY 15m, 2026-06-11

Pattern tags:
- `session-regime-shift`

Context:

Operational takeaway:

## Case 3: BTC-USD hourly, 2026-06-12

Pattern tags:
- `session-regime-shift`
- `repair-rally`

Context:
- BTC shifted between sharp intraday repair rallies and renewed hesitation around the `63-64k` area

Why it matters:
- crypto sessions do not have a clean cash open and close, but intraday regime shifts still occur

Operational takeaway:
- continuous markets still require the same discipline: trend behavior can degrade back into range behavior quickly
