# Standard Cases

## Case 1: Breakout Pullback in Trend

Context:
- market already behaves like trend
- breakout is strong
- first pullback is small

Correct interpretation:
- breakout pullback continuation

Operational takeaway:
- first pullback often gives better risk than the breakout bar itself

## Case 2: Low 2 in Bear Trend

Context:
- bearish context remains intact
- first rally attempt fails
- second rally ends with clearer signal

Correct interpretation:
- low 2 continuation short

Operational takeaway:
- second entries often give cleaner information than first ones
