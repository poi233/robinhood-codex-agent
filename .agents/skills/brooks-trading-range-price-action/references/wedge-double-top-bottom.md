# Wedge, Double Top, Double Bottom

## Wedge

A wedge is usually a three-push structure. It can signal either:

- exhaustion against the prior move
- a pullback ending in the direction of the dominant trend

## Double Top / Double Bottom

These are location-sensitive patterns. They become useful when they appear:

- at a range edge
- after a climactic move
- near a magnet

## What Matters Most

The pattern alone is not enough. Ask:

- trend or range?
- edge or middle?
- first signal or second signal?
- room to target?

## Common Error

Trading shape without location.
