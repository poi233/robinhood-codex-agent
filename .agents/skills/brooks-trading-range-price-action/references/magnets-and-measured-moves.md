# Magnets and Measured Moves

## Magnets

Markets gravitate toward obvious prior prices:

- prior highs or lows
- gap areas
- moving averages
- range extremes
- measured move projections

## Why Magnets Matter

They shape both target selection and failure risk. A breakout may not be a true trend start; it may only be a vacuum move toward a magnet.

## Measured Moves

Use them as conditional targets, not promises.

Best use:

- project target if trend behavior stays one-sided

Bad use:

- demand a measured move even after overlap returns

## Common Error

Ignoring nearby magnets when evaluating reward-to-risk.
