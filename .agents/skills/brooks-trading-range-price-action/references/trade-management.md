# Trade Management

## Entry Is Not Enough

A good read can still become a bad trade if the stop, target, or regime downgrade is wrong.

## Stops

Use stops that match context:

- beyond the signal bar for tactical setups
- beyond the swing point for structure trades
- beyond the opposite side of the range for range fades

## Targets

Use at least two:

- nearest logical magnet
- extended target if trend behavior persists

## Downgrade Rule

If follow-through stalls and overlap returns, reassess the market as a range.

## Scaling

Scaling can be useful, but only if it follows a pre-decided plan. Do not scale because of hope.

## Common Error

Holding a breakout swing thesis after the market has already become two-sided again.
