# Breakout Quality

## Strong Breakout Traits

- large trend body
- small tails
- closes near the extreme
- next two or three bars follow through
- nearby support or resistance is clearly violated
- pullbacks stay small

## Weak Breakout Traits

- large tails
- immediate hesitation
- closes near the middle
- no follow-through
- still inside larger range structure

## Trading Implication

Strong breakout:
- can justify continuation entries

Weak breakout:
- often better treated as likely failure or a setup for pullback-only participation

## Common Error

Buying the close of a strong-looking bar without checking whether it is just hitting the top of a bigger range.
