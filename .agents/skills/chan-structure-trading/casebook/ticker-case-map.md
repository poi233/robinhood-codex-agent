- false third-buy risk

- multi-timeframe execution discipline

Best used for:

- late-stage exhaustion

- climactic highs into earnings

## NVDA

Best used for:

- leader-stock pullback analysis
- early bounce versus real continuation
- false third-buy situations in strong names after structure damage

## AAPL

Best used for:

- clean type 3 continuation

- daily thesis versus intraday noise discipline
