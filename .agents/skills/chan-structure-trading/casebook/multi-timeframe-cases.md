# Multi-Timeframe Cases

## Case 1: Daily Third Buy, Intraday Precision

Context:
- daily chart shows valid continuation after center breakout
- intraday chart offers a cleaner retest trigger

Correct interpretation:
- daily chart owns the thesis
- intraday chart owns the timing

Operational takeaway:
- use lower levels to improve entry, not to rewrite the trade

## Case 2: Intraday Long Against Weekly Ceiling

Context:
- lower level shows a bullish trigger
- higher level is running directly into major balance resistance

Correct interpretation:
- tactical long may exist, but the higher level limits follow-through expectations

Operational takeaway:
- target size and holding period must respect higher-level structure
