# Multi-Timeframe Discipline Cases

## Case 1: SPY, daily repair vs intraday long, 2026-06-12

Pattern tags:
- `multi-timeframe-conflict`
- `trend-to-range-downgrade`

Context:
- daily chart was still repairing sharp damage from the `760` area
- intraday chart on `2026-06-12` offered a clean long from the `740` zone

Correct interpretation:
- intraday long was valid as a tactical trade
- daily chart did not yet justify treating it as a full resumed swing trend

Operational takeaway:
- lower-timeframe opportunity does not automatically upgrade to higher-timeframe conviction

## Case 2: BTC-USD, hourly bounce inside daily damage, 2026-06-07 to 2026-06-12

Pattern tags:
- `multi-timeframe-conflict`
- `repair-rally`

Context:
- hourly chart produced tradable rebounds
- daily chart remained in post-breakdown repair

Correct interpretation:
- lower-level longs could be traded, but the daily structure still argued against overconfident continuation claims

Operational takeaway:
- trade the level you chose; do not use a lower-level win to rewrite a higher-level thesis

## Case 3: AAPL, daily continuation with intraday noise, 2026-05-01 to 2026-05-22

Pattern tags:
- `multi-timeframe-conflict`
- `type3-continuation`

Context:
- daily chart stayed in constructive continuation
- intraday pullbacks were frequent and noisy

Correct interpretation:

Operational takeaway:
- time horizon discipline protects good higher-level setups from lower-level overreaction
