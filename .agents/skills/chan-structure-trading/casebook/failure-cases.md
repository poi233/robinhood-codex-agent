# Failure Cases

## Case 1: False Third Buy

Observed setup:
- trader sees breakout from center
- enters on pullback
- later price falls back into the old overlap

Why it failed:
- the old center was not actually left behind

Correct interpretation:
- center extension, not continuation

Operational takeaway:
- re-entry into center cancels the clean third-buy thesis

## Case 2: Forced Divergence

Observed setup:
- analyst wants a reversal
- indicator looks weaker
- price still behaves like late but intact trend

Why it failed:
- divergence was imposed before structural failure

Operational takeaway:
- force comparison must be tied to completed legs
