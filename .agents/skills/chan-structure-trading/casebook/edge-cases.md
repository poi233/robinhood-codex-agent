# Edge Cases

## Case 1: Apparent Third Buy Near Center Boundary

Context:
- price appears to leave the center
- pullback comes very close to the old boundary

Why it was tricky:
- continuation and re-entry risk coexist

Correct interpretation:
- treat as borderline until the boundary is clearly respected

Operational takeaway:
- when in doubt, downgrade to observe

## Case 2: Lower-Level Type 2 Inside Higher-Level Repair

Context:
- lower level prints a neat retest pattern
- higher level is still inside damaged structure

Correct interpretation:
- lower-level setup is not enough to declare higher-level reversal

Operational takeaway:
- lower-level beauty does not repair higher-level damage
