# Standard Cases

## Case 1: Clean Third Buy

Context:
- higher level in uptrend
- execution level forms a center
- price leaves the center upward
- pullback holds above the center

Why it looked valid:
- continuation already declared
- pullback did not re-enter balance

Correct interpretation:
- type 3 buy

Operational takeaway:
- do not chase the first breakout bar
- the pullback hold is the cleaner continuation entry

## Case 2: Type 1 Exhaustion Reversal

Context:
- old trend extends to a final extreme
- new extreme has visibly weaker force
- first reaction away from the extreme is sharp

Why it looked valid:
- exhaustion happened at the edge of an already mature move

Correct interpretation:
- type 1 reversal attempt

Operational takeaway:
- early entries need tighter discipline than continuation trades
